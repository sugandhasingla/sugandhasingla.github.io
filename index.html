Research Experience

Resiliency Considerations in Biomass to Biofuels Supply Chain Design OptimisationJuly 2014- Mar 2015

Research Assistant, Department of Chemical Engineering, IIT Bombay
Guide: Prof Yogendra Shastri
  • Developed a Deterministic Optimization Model using Mixed Integer Linear Programming in GAMS which provides optimal supply chain network design for lignocellulosic biofuels produced in 13 County region of Southern Illinois, USA.
  • Incorporated the theoretical concept of Regional Biomass Processing Depots (RBPDs) to quantitatively analyse and show their importance in reducing transport costs, acting as efficient storage locations and in turn, mitigating losses incurred due to disruptions in network such as loss of feed due to calamities like floods, drought etc.
  • Developed a Stochastic Optimization Model to incorporate disruptions costs accounting for uncertainties in network by adding probable eventualities of the uncertainties in the model. The model provides the Optimized Supply Chain Design with quantized efficiency-resiliency trade-offs. Ananlysis shows substantial improvements in reliability can be made with marginal rise in operating cost without decreasing efficiency very much.
  • Comparisons made between robustness of supply chains obtained from consideration and non-consideration of expected disruptions shows that substantial improvements in reliability can be made with marginal rise in operating cost without decreasing efficiency very much

Data Reconciliation and Gross Error Analysis of Self Powered Neutron DetectorsApr 2015 - Present

Research Assistant, Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Bombay and Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, Mumbai, India
Guide: Prof Mani Bhushan and Prof Madhu Bellur
  • Grouped SPNDs with different dynamic characteristics into smaller clusters of SPNDs with higher correlation coefficients using K-Means Clustering Algorithms in MATLAB.
  • Developed models using Principle Component Analysis based on Eigen-Decomposition of Covariance matrix of measurement data of SPNDs in clusters. The models are used for fault detection and diagnosis in defective SPND if any, based on measurement data from other SPNDs in the same clusters.
